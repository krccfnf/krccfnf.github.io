Sappho 47

...Ἔρος δ' ἐτίναξέ μοι
φρένας, ὠς ἄνεμος κὰτ ὄρος δρύσιν ἐμπέτων.

Eros shook my heart
a wind
on a mountain
rushing down upon the trees.

Originally posted to this website on _____ for Pride Month. The Sapphic fragments are given their standard Lobel-Paige numbers. The Greek is taken from there, per Inadmidst. The Greek from the /Anthology/ is taken from Perseus.
