orange hawkweed

by Matthew Stokdyk

glowing coals in the green
bow their fuzzy heads
under the moon
collecting tongues


into rubbery jade grey skin
making the front lawn
more spectacle
than a barnfire.

Originally published in issue 28.1 of Hummingbird.
